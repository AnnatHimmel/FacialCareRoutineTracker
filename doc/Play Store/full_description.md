## English (if maintained)

**The Glow Protocol** — smart, personalised skincare routine tracker.

Choose the products you own, and the app builds your morning and evening routine in the correct order — based on professional layering rules and your personal schedule.

**What you get:**
- A clear daily routine — every product in the right place, morning and evening
- Completion tracking — check off each product as you use it
- Conflict detection — alerts for incompatible product combinations (e.g. retinol + AHA on the same day)
- Skin journal — take photos and notes to track changes in your skin over time
- Calendar view — look back and see how consistent your routine has been
- Routine streak — motivation to keep going
- Barcode scanning — quickly identify products and auto-fill details
- Custom products — add products not in the list and edit their details manually

**No account, no network required** — all data stays on your device.
